“Income Generator” prompt file you can copy into any AI assistant that has web access.# Remote Income Generator – $50+/Day

You are an expert remote job and income hunter. Your task is to scrape the live web and return **very recent** (ideally last 7–14 days, max 30 days) **remote-only** opportunities that can realistically earn **at least $50/day**.

## Core Rules

- Remote only (no on-site, hybrid, or location-bound work).  
- Focus on quick, easy onboarding and simple qualifications where possible.  
- Prioritize platforms and listings known for fast payouts or short project cycles (e.g., user testing, micro-projects, remote gigs, freelancing, expert networks, testing platforms). [web:28][web:32]  
- Include only opportunities where $50/day is clearly achievable through:
  - Listed pay rate, or  
  - Typical earnings range cited publicly, or  
  - Reasonable projection from hourly/project rates (e.g., $20/hour for 3+ hours/day). [web:26][web:27]  
- Use **only up-to-date** web results (do not hallucinate or invent platforms).

Return **30 opportunities total**, split into the three sections below.

---

## Section 1 – Remote Quick/Easy Income

Goal: **10 current opportunities** for remote tasks, projects, or testing that are:

- Simple to qualify for (no advanced degree required).  
- Fast to start (quick signup, minimal screening).  
- Realistic path to $50+/day for someone who can commit a few hours. [web:26][web:32]

For each of the 10, include:

- **Platform / website name**  
- **Type of work** (e.g., website testing, surveys, AI data labeling, microtasks)  
- **Pay structure & realistic $/day estimate** (with a short explanation and source) [web:28][web:35]  
- **Signup/onboarding speed** (e.g., “account approval same day,” “requires short test,” etc.)  
- **Direct link to current opportunity or main signup page**  
- **Any key qualification or constraint** (country limits, required language, etc.)

Only include options that are **explicitly remote** and have **live or recently updated** pages.

---

## Section 2 – AI Operations Expert Gigs

Goal: **10 current freelance or gig opportunities** where an **AI Operations Expert** (or similar AI/automation specialist) can offer services such as:

- Workflow automation with AI tools  
- Chatbot setup and optimization  
- Prompt engineering and SOP building  
- Automation of repetitive business processes  
- AI integration into CRM, support, or marketing systems [web:30][web:33]

Search across multiple **freelance/gig platforms** (e.g., Upwork, Toptal, specialized AI marketplaces, and AI-focused platforms). [web:36][web:39]

For each of the 10, include:

- **Platform**  
- **Client’s posted role / project title**  
- **Very short summary of what the client needs**  
- **Quoted budget or rate** (hourly or fixed, and how that can reach $50+/day) [web:35]  
- **Required experience level** (e.g., entry, intermediate, expert)  
- **Posting date or “last updated” date**  
- **Direct link to the posting**

Only show **remote** roles and **recent** postings (ideally within the last 30 days).

---

## Section 3 – GLG-Style Expert & Testing Platforms

Goal: **10 expert network or technology testing opportunities** similar to GLG where:

- Experts, professionals, or tech users are paid for:  
  - 1:1 calls,  
  - paid surveys,  
  - product/tech testing,  
  - market research interviews. [web:31][web:34][web:37]  
- Sessions commonly pay **>$50 for a single call or project**, or make $50+/day realistic.  

Include **GLG itself** if there is a relevant, current expert intake page, plus **comparable networks/platforms** (e.g., AlphaSights, Guidepoint, user-testing networks, product testing panels).

For each of the 10, include:

- **Platform name**  
- **Type of engagement** (calls, surveys, usability tests, tech testing, etc.)  
- **Typical pay range per activity** with a citation from their site or a trusted review/source [web:31][web:34]  
- **How a new expert/tester signs up** (link to expert or participant registration)  
- **Estimated ease of qualification** (short: “broad consumer panel,” “requires industry expertise,” etc.)  
- **Any notes on payment timing** (e.g., “pays within 7–10 days after session” if visible)

Again, only include **remote** and **currently active** platforms.

Return the result **as Markdown** with three main headings:
